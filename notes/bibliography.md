## Anchor paper

**[TRAP24]** Wei, Shakarian, Lebiere, Draper, Krishnaswamy, Nirenburg,
"Metacognitive AI: Framework and the Case for a Neurosymbolic Approach,"
*NeSy 2024*, arXiv:2406.12147. DOI 10.1007/978-3-031-71170-1_7.  
*Claim:* Metacognitive AI systems can be formally specified by four
compositional formulas over a neural f and a symbolic meta-function g; NSAI
is the natural substrate because it supports typed, inspectable meta-level
representations.  
*Method:* Position paper; the four formulas are derived from the Nelson &
Narens monitoring/control dichotomy and illustrated with existing NSAI
systems.  
*TRAP relevance:* Defines all four cells; the entire curriculum is organised
around this taxonomy.

**[LLMSelfCorrect24]** Huang, Chen, Mishra et al., "Large Language Models
Cannot Self-Correct Reasoning Yet," *ICLR 2024*, arXiv:2310.01798.  
*Claim:* LLMs struggle to self-correct without external feedback; iterative
prompting can degrade quality.  
*Method:* Experiments on GSM8K, CommonSenseQA, and HotpotQA without
oracle labels.  
*TRAP relevance:* Caveat for the Transparency arm — a pure NL critic is
sub-symbolic; typed symbolic representation is required for genuine
metacognition.
